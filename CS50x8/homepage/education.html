University of Zanjan

Master's degree

Zanjan University is one of the oldest and most prestigious public universities under the supervision of the Ministry of Science, Research and Technology, located in the city of Zanjan. This university currently has 5 faculties, 470 faculty members and more than 10 thousand students. This university is the second largest university in Iran after Isfahan University of Technology


In 1353, the Council for the Development of Higher Education approved the establishment of the Higher School of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry in the fields of agricultural management and animal husbandry in Zanjan city. Following that, on March 14, 2013, the first meeting of the Board of Trustees of Zanjan High School was held and it was decided to start its educational activity from October 1, 2014 by accepting 72 students through the national entrance exam in the fields of agricultural management and animal husbandry management. In this way, the educational work of Zanjan University (High School of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry) officially started in 1354.
